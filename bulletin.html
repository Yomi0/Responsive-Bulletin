I dare to call him father

Before I was born He knew my name, He gave me breath and life abundantly, He prospered the works of my hands and multiplied me, then spoke saying "I have arrived".

He beckoned to me and I turned a deaf ear. He knocked at the door of my heart but I refused to answer. I wallowed in sin and the flesh took me, I thought there wasn't any more to life and that I had it all.

Then all of a sudden the cares of this world enveloped me, my days were dreary and my nights long and scary, my fears shook me and I began to lose all I had.

Then I remembered that He was still at the door, knocking and waiting. I turned to him and called him "FATHER", and he turned to me and said "Son, I have always been here." I wept and said "Lord, I am sorry.". He said to me "Welcome to the fold." From then on I call Him, FATHER.
